Learning Opportunities!

Small group 3+

Learn the basics of setting up your poles, adjusting height and pole tips and getting used to the grip and balance. Learn poling techniques for rehabilitation/recovery or urban poling for increased physical outcomes. Practice walking with a natural stride using alternate arm and leg swing. Explore walking indoors and on paved surfaces, grass, and gravel. Learn to step up and down from curbs and on stairs…that's right, stairs!

11:00 a.m., held at LaGrua Center on 2nd, 3rd and 4th Fridays of the month.
