Nordic Walking
for a healthy today and an independent tomorrow

Are you curious about Nordic Walking and how it can help you stay strong and maintain your independence?
Nordic Walking is a total body exercise that can reduce back and joint pain, improve posture,
manage chronic disease including arthritis, prevent falls and improve your confidence and skill while hiking trails!
